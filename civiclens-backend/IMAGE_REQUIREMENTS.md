## üìù **Image Guidelines**

### Quality Requirements:
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Size**: 500KB - 5MB per image
- **Orientation**: Mix of portrait and landscape as appropriate

### Content Requirements:
1. **Clear & Focused**: Issue should be clearly visible
2. **Context**: Show surroundings for location identification
3. **Realistic**: Actual civic issues, not staged
4. **Variety**: Different angles, distances, lighting conditions
5. **Safety**: All images taken safely (no dangerous locations)

### Naming Convention:
Format: `{complaint_id}_{category}_{description}.jpg`

Examples:
- `01_pothole_closeup.jpg`
- `02_water_burst_pipe.jpg`
- `03_garbage_overflowing.jpg`
